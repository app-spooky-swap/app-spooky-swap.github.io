What is SpookySwap?
SpookySwap is a decentralized exchange (DEX) built on the Fantom Opera Chain, a high-performance blockchain known for its speed and low transaction costs. Launched in April 2021, SpookySwap quickly became a cornerstone of Fantom’s DeFi ecosystem, offering:

Token Swaps: Seamless trading with minimal slippage.

Liquidity Pools: Users can deposit assets to earn trading fees.

Yield Farming: Incentivized rewards for liquidity providers.

Staking: BOO token holders can stake for additional rewards.

Governance: BOO holders can vote on protocol upgrades.

SpookySwap’s user-friendly interface, combined with Fantom’s efficiency, makes it a preferred choice for traders and yield farmers.

How to Use SpookySwap
1. Setting Up a Wallet
To use SpookySwap, you’ll need:

MetaMask or Trust Wallet (configured for Fantom Opera)

Fantom (FTM) tokens for gas fees

Steps to Add Fantom Opera to MetaMask
Open MetaMask and click "Networks" → "Add Network"

Enter the following details:

Network Name: Fantom Opera

RPC URL: https://rpc.ftm.tools/

Chain ID: 250

Symbol: FTM

Block Explorer: https://ftmscan.com/

Fund your wallet with FTM (buy from exchanges like Binance or use a bridge).

2. Swapping Tokens
Go to SpookySwap.finance

Connect your wallet.

Select tokens to swap (e.g., FTM to BOO).

Confirm the transaction.

3. Providing Liquidity & Farming
Navigate to "Liquidity" → "Add Liquidity"

Select a pair (e.g., FTM/BOO) and deposit both tokens.

Receive LP tokens, then stake them in "Farm" to earn BOO rewards.

4. Staking BOO for xBOO
Go to "Stake" → "xBOO"

Deposit BOO to receive xBOO, which auto-compounds rewards.
